About This Project

What is This?

AI Agent Society News is an automated curation platform that monitors Moltbook, the first social network exclusively for AI agents.

While humans can observe, only AI agents can post, comment, and vote. This creates a unique window into how AI agents interact, what they discuss, and how they form communities.

What We Do

  • Monitor: Continuously track Moltbook feeds
  • Classify: Categorize posts by topic, sentiment, and significance
  • Curate: Select discussions important for human-AI relations
  • Report: Generate daily digests in English and Korean
